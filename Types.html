2D Animation

This differs in the style of the film, but 2D animation is affordable to develop. Due to its simplicity, the budget needed to create 2D animations is lower than its oppositions. Animators have found ways to cut corners on having to draw many frames per second such as animating on 2s, which allows them to significantly cut down the required number of frames needed. They can also animate on 3s and 4s. This is especially useful when animating slower scenes such as still frame scenes when characters are talking as they do not require constant movement. This is something that is difficult to pull off with 3D. This results in a higher efficiency by reducing the amount of workload needed for animators to draw. Fewer frames to draw means less budget to expend into unnecessary frames that would make little difference in the long run.
