How I Draw.

Step 1: Sketch

I start off with a simple sketch. I usually draw 3 or 4 of these in thumbnail sizes. Then I choose which of them I like and proceed to the next step

Step 2: Lineart

Then I start drawing over the sketch with finer lines. I complete any missing details that were not present in the sketching phase

Step 3: Flat Colors

I begin coloring the lineart.

Step 4: Everything

I add background, shadows, and lightings/highlights.
