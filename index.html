A career in animation could be one of the best decisions in a person’s life or it could be the worst. It is one of the most consumed media in modern entertainment with the advancement of television and the internet. Kids growing up in today’s age are also wishing to become animators in the future. Many young and impressionable people are looking to find ways to make a living doing animation. It is also fairly easy to get into creating animations and learning. The industry also provides a plethora of job positions and opportunities. However, the industry itself is also plagued with its own negatives. Overwork, low pay, and high competition may overshadow the positives that comes with this choice of career.
